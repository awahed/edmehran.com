FAQ

Common questions

A few answers to help you understand when a fractional CTO or an AI enablement engagement makes sense.

What does a fractional CTO do?

A fractional CTO provides senior technical leadership without a full-time hire. I help with architecture decisions, prioritization, delivery planning, and hands-on execution where it actually matters.

When should I hire a fractional CTO instead of a full-time one?

When you need senior direction quickly, but hiring full-time is too early, too risky, or too slow. Fractional support works especially well during product launches, rebuilds, scaling phases, and periods of uncertainty.

What does a technical & AI assessment include?

A focused review of your product, processes, and systems to identify risks, priorities, and where AI actually adds value. You get clear recommendations and a concrete roadmap, so you can invest with confidence.

What is an AI enablement sprint?

A time-boxed sprint to help your team adopt AI and automation in real workflows. It typically includes tool setup, best practices, and practical coaching on your own projects to improve speed and quality.

Do you work in German- and Italian-speaking Switzerland?

Yes. I work in English, Italian, and German, and I regularly support teams across Switzerland and Germany.

Do I need a Chief AI Officer (CAIO)?

Not necessarily. Many companies — especially SMEs — don't need a dedicated CAIO. What they need is someone who can evaluate where AI creates real value, integrate it into existing workflows, and guide the team through adoption. That's what I do as part of my advisory and AI enablement work.

Can you help us show up in Google and AI tools like ChatGPT?

This is not about tricks or prompt hacks. It is about making your website a reliable, citable source.

Yes. I help teams make their websites easier for search engines and AI systems such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity to discover, understand, and reference. This starts with a focused audit covering technical SEO, structured data, entity clarity, and content positioning. Based on that, I deliver a prioritized action plan and, if needed, hands-on implementation to turn visibility into qualified leads, not just traffic.
